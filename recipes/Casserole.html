Green Bean Casserole

Description

A green bean casserole makes the Thanksgiving holiday complete, and this

version can be on the table in no time at all thanks to just four ingredients from

your pantry or fridge: canned green beans, condensed mushroom soup, French-

fried onions, and shredded Cheddar cheese.

Ingredient

  • Green Beans: This easy green bean casserole starts with two drained cans of

    green beans.

  • Canned Soup A can of condensed cream of mushroom soup creates a

    creamy texture and adds savory flavor.

  • Cheese This recipe calls for shredded Cheddar cheese. Use sharp, mild, or a

    blend of both.

  • French-Fried Onion The green beans casserole gets its crunch from a

    French-fried onion topping
