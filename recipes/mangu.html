Mangu

Description

is made up of boiled green plantains with or without peel on. When cooked through and soft the peel is removed. Plantains are then

mashed with a fork and some water in which they were boiled in. The goal is to mash the plantains until it becomes smooth with no

lumps. The dish is topped with saut√©ed red onions that have been cooked with vinegar and oil.

Ingredients

here are the ingredients needed.
  • Picked red onions
  • Green Plantains
  • Butter, softened to room temperature
  • Water + additional water for boiling the plantains
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil the plantains.
  2. Mash the plantains
  3. Fry the onion
  4. Serve
